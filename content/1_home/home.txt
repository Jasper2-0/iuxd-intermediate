Headertitle: #LET'S XD TOGETHER

----

Headertext: Adobe XD is a User Experience design tool for web and mobile apps. It's available for macOS and Windows. The app is vector-based (paths). There is also an Adobe XD mobile app. The tool is particularly used to create wireframes/designs and simple interactive clickable prototypes. It's completely free and easy to use, even for beginners.

Whytitle: ###WHY ADOBE XD?

----

Whytextone: Even though Adobe XD is still relatively new, it's becoming one of the best design tools on the market. With new updates all the time and great features. XD Seperates itself from other tools by their features like advanced animations, voice triggers and many more. Adobe XD makes it super-easy to quickly mockup a low-fidelity wireframe. Being able to design low-fidelity wireframes quickly means that we can focus on creating a layout that offers a terrific user experience.

----

Whytexttwo: Since Adobe XD has an option to collaborate, it makes it really easy for teams to share their work. You can also share your designs with customers. The app is great for prototyping with users aswell. XD Is linked to Adobe resources, so you can implement Adobe Fonts, icons and many more. XD offers a variety of many plugins, like stock photo's, calendars and many more. Adobe XD is focused on what the designers and users really need.

Whotitle: ###WHO USES ADOBE XD?

----

Whotextone: Adobe XD is great for all type of designers, like the ones working in a creative agency. It's also great for individual use, students and start-ups since it's free. Many design agencies use XD in order to test their products with their target audience. The tool is perfectly fit for teamwork. Since it's easy to learn XD, it's for beginners aswell as experts.

----

Whotexttwo: Adobe XD is used during a large part of the design proces, usually in the middle and end of the proces. In these phases the concept is being tranformed into a high-fidelity prototype. During different iterations the product can be tested and evaluated. At the end the product can be realised and transferred to developers easily.
